New Practice Times

Hopefully by now, everyone is aware of the new extended practice times.  On the 1st, 2nd and 3rd Saturdays of each month, practice sessions will commence from 9:30am until 4:00pm.  A minimum of 1 x DTL and 1 x Skeet layout will be open as other layouts maybe in use for corporate of novice shooting sessions.  On the 4th Saturday each month (the day preceeding a sporting competition) the practice layouts will not open until 12:00 to allow for safety of our staff setting up the sporting traps.

New Committee
