CCTC NEW PRACTICE TIMES

Thank you to those people who completed the recent survey about the club.  We appreciate the feedback and the wealth of positive comments, acknowledgements and thank yous that we received was very rewarding. 

Based on that feedback, we are very pleased to announce that practice opening hours will be extended as follows:
  • 1st, 2nd & 3rd Saturday's of each month = 9.30 am to 4.00pm
  • 4th Saturday of each month = 12.00 to 4.00 pm

On the 1st, 2nd and 3rd Saturdays, only 1 x skeet and 1 x DTL layout will be open from 9.30 am until midday.  This allows us to accommodate the novice and corporate groups on the other layouts.

There will be no extension to practice times on the 4th Saturday to allow the sporting guys the time to safely set up for the following days' competition.

There is no change to unlicensed shooter times.

To keep shooting costs down, the club is run purely by volunteers and these extended practice hours are only available to you because of the continued dedication and hard work of a small number of people.  Less than 1% of our members are regular helpers at the club. 

Remember that volunteering and helping out, even if only for an hour or so, is guaranteed to improve your shooting (laws of karma!).

Happy shooting
