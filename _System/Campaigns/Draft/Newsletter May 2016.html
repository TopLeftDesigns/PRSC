 User Pays or Volunteers?

 Sporting clubs are usually run on one of two systems; " User Pays" or  "Volunteer".

We run our club on a volunteer basis. All members (including volunteers) pay the same practice fees, membership fees and nomination fees. However, our volunteers run the office, bar, kitchen, load traps, set up and do all of the administration. This is why we need to enlist the help of ALL of our members to help. Where you are unable to assist in one area, we greatly appreciate your help in another.

As much as we would all like to come along and "just shoot", remember that every time anyone does a little bit of something to help, they are keeping the practice and nomination fees low, not just for themselves , but their shooting buddies as well.

As such, we appreciate each and everyone of you!

New Caretaker

Please welcome Ron Bischof as our new on-site cartaker.  Ron is a keen DTL shooter and has been a member and committee member of the club for sometime.  Ron and Dee have spent many hours on the mowers and doing the cleaning and getting the club looking great again.

Change to Practice Opening Times

Based on feedback from our survey, we have stretched our hardworking volunteers a little further by offering Saturday opening from 9.30am to 4.00pm (this excludes the 4th Saturday prior to sporting due to set up requirements).  During the morning session a minimum of 1 skeet and 1 DTL layout only will be open while group and novice sessions are held on the other layouts.

We hope to move to a weekday opening later in the year....... watch this space!

Skeet Voice Release System

We have purchased 3 skeet voice release systems.  This is so that you do not need someone on the buttons for you at practice and also so that 'refereeing' is much easier for our novice/elderly competition shooters. This simple and easy to use system replaces the conventional cord and should make life easier.

Speed Limit

Please observe the 10km per hour speed limit on the driveway entrance.  This will avoid excessive wear on the driveway.  We would much rather spend money on improving the shooting facilities than on the drive way...

We can't contain ourselves any longer!

After many years of use it is time to say goodbye to the three equipment containers adjacent to the childrens’ playground. The club is replacing them one with a 3 bay shed. The tidy up has begun, the containers are being removed this week and the erection of the shed will commence shortly.

More communication and security

We are expanding our security coverage on the grounds and also installing a suitable tannoy system.
This will enable us to provide better security at all times and improved communication during practise and competition.

ISSF Skeet

The committee is investigating the possibility of adapting one of the three skeet layouts to be able to cater for the increase in demand for ISSF (Olympic) skeet to be shot at the club….more information to follow.

Tower Power

The installation of a third trap onto the sporting tower means we are now able to offer even more variety for our 5-stand and sporting practise and competition - thanks to everyone involved - especially those who braved the heights to carry out the work!
