Website Documentation - BC Site Technical Guide

This document provides technical descriptions of how your Business Catalyst site has been developed and should be used by the site owner or their web master as a reference for making custom changes or understanding how the particular features of BC have been implemented for this particular website

  • created: 10/10/2014
  • latest update: 06/11/2016
  • by: Greg Tomkins

Log Support Ticket Email Support Ticket

This site should provide you with sufficient detail to allow you to understand how the different features incorporated into your website have been implemented by way of BC modules. The details provided are for your own particular website and assume you already have a working knowledge in the use of Business Catalyst.
We do not encourage clients to make changes to the layouts in any site templates or module layouts unless you have a deep understanding of HTML and CSS and invariably, some understanding of Javascript. Making such changes will impact many parts of your site and can lead to serious problems.
If you are at all unsure about what you want to do, please contact us through the support portal (see buttons above) or phone us on 0419 706 325. All change requests or problems with your site MUST be logged by way of a support ticket by either logging into our support portal or by sending an email to us via the above button.

Data / Content Types

Your site is comprised of different data that makes up your overall site. The sorts of data held in yru BC site to which you have access is:

  • Site Templates for controlling how yur overal lpages ae presented in a consistent manner
  • Module templates that control how data held in specific BC Modules are presented
  • System Pages and email layouts & content
  • static web pages
  • Web Forms used for capture of information on different pages of your site
  • Content Holders for commonly used HTML code in any part of your site
  • CSS files that control the style settings for the content presented
  • JS or Javascript files that provide certain processing of page content within your browser
  • Images and Videos used witin your site's pages and modules
  • CRM data
  • Web Apps which ae custom databases utilised witin your site
  • Module data
  • eCommerce related data arund products and product catalogs, tax and shipping
  • Site Settings
