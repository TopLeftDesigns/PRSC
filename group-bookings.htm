Group Bookings

We welcome group bookings at the Pine Rivers Shooting Club. We ask that you allow us 1 – 2 weeks’ notice for your event. Have a look at our calendar and select one of our shooting nights then send us an email and we will get back to you to confirm your booking.

We have group rates available for groups of 4 or more shooters.

Group Rate - $40 per person

This includes:-

  • Range Fees
  • Firearms handling and safety instruction.
  • Your own Range Officer to assist you for the night
  • Use of club gun and ammunition,
  • Eye & ear protection and a fluro visitors vest.
  • A target.
  • Tea and coffee.

Group rates are ideal for the families, friends, Birthday Parties, organisations and clubs.
