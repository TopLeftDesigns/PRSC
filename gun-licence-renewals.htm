GUN LICENCE RENEWALS FOR MEMBERS

Qld Weapons Licensing Branch sends your Licence Renewal Notification to you about one to two months prior to your Licence expiring. This notification will have a Reference Code.

AS SOON AS you receive this notification request from Weapons Licensing, complete the downloadable form and return it to the club's Weapons Licensing Branch Liaisons or complete the details in the form below.

Allow at least 2 weeks prior to the Expiry date for this to be processed.

The club must supply Weapons Licensing with a Declaration regarding your Membership status. This Declaration has a 28 day expiry from date of signing.

This means the club will need to know the date by which you will need to have the information back to Weapons Licensing. This enables the Declaration to be produced and supplied within a time frame that does not expire prior to your lodgement of the renewal.
