Practice sessions are available as follows:

  •    1st, 2nd and 3rd Saturdays of each month from 9.30: am until 4:00 pm
  •    4th Saturday of each month, from 12.00pm until 4.00 pm.

Please note that only 1 x DTL and 1 x Skeet layout will be available for the mornings of the 1st, 2nd and 3rd Saturdays.

Traps are operated by tokens which are available from the clubhouse at the cost of $8 for members or $10 for non-members plus a $10 range fee (non members only).

Competitions are held on Sundays and members and non members are welcome to attend.

The monthly competition schedule is:

Licensed shooters only. Shooters must wear closed-in shoes.
