Online Membership Renewal Checklist

The Pine Rivers Shooting Club has a responsibility to report to Weapons Licensing QLD as required by them. The following details may be requested by the Weapons Licensing QLD so please fill in all the sections. Some of the information requested may be for the benefit of record keeping by the PRSCi. We have compiled this check list to assist you.

You will not be able to complete your renewal if mandatory fields are not completed.

  • Read the PRSCi's Drug and Alcohol Policy tick the box to accept this Policy. We are not able to accept your renewal without this being accepted
  • Scan and Upload your completed Statement of Participation (Form QP0515).
  • If you have a category H Licence and own a firearm, attach a copy of your Participation Record or documentation explaining why you have not been able to meet your competition requirements.
  • Scan and upload copies of your current firearms licences.
  • Scan and upload and SSAA range cards that you have to be able to claim your discounted range fee.
